## **What I'd want to know from backtesting:**

- Win rate at 2R — is the structural stop too wide or too tight on average
- Time of day distribution — do setups cluster around certain hours
- Average R at which trades close — are most wins just barely 2R or do they run further

## **Potential weaknesses to watch:**

- In trending markets this model will get chopped — sweeps happen but reversals don't follow through
- The structural stop can be very wide on volatile days making the 2R target hard to reach
- FVG entries work best with displacement — if the FVG forms on weak momentum it may not hold
