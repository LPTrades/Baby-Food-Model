## Why it's powerful:
 I can connect:
	- experiments
	- problems
	- ideas
	- models
	- architecture
into a research network.

## Intermal links
### What [[]] does 
- Creates links to another note.
- Think of it like a Wikipedia-style connection between ideas.
### Example
- Imagine I discover a bug. Inside my daily log I will write:
  ```
  Found duplicate FVG issue in [[Baby Food Model 3]].
  Created note in [[Problems]].
  ```
- Now both notes are connected.
## Backlinks
If 20 notes mention ```Baby Food Model 3 ``` then Obsidian automatically shows:
- every note connected to it.
That means I can instantly see:
- experiments
- logs
- bugs
- ideas
- architecture notes
related to the model.
